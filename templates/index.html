About Us

Mphumza Safety was founded with a vision to create a secure environment for students and provide parents with peace of mind. Our platform offers innovative solutions for tracking incidents, ensuring real-time reporting, and promoting transparency in school safety.

We believe in using technology to enhance security and empower both parents and school administrators in safeguarding the well-being of students.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a safer learning environment by leveraging technology to provide real-time incident reporting, prevention strategies, and intervention tools.

We are committed to creating a seamless communication channel between parents, students, and school administrators to enhance safety and well-being.
